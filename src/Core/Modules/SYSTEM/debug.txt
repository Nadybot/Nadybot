To view the current loglevels:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loglevel<end>

To temporary change the loglevel(s):
<tab><highlight><symbol>loglevel 'channel' 'loglevel'<end>

'channel' also accepts wildcars. So These all work:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loglevel * warning<end>
<tab><highlight><symbol>loglevel RELAY_MODULE/* debug<end>
<tab><highlight><symbol>loglevel RELAY_MODULE/RelayProtocol/* debug<end>
<tab><highlight><symbol>loglevel Core/* info<end>

To reset your temporarily changed loglevels back to your configuration:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loglevel reset<end>

To debug a single command execution and upload logs for inspection:
<tab><highlight><symbol>debug 'command with params'<end>
<tab>e.g. <highlight><symbol>debug whois Nady<end>
