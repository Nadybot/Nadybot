To see a list of all currently issued tokens:
<tab><highlight><symbol>apiauth<end>

To create a new token for yourself:
<tab><highlight><symbol>apiauth create<end>

To delete one of your tokens:
<tab><highlight><symbol>apiauth delete 'token'<end>

To reset the last used sequence for one of your tokens:
<tab><highlight><symbol>apiauth reset 'token'<end>
