To get a list of all your mappings org rank to bot access level:
<tab><highlight><symbol>maprank<end>

To give admin rights to org rank 0 (President, Monarch, Lorg, etc.) :
<tab><highlight><symbol>maprank 0 to admin<end>

To remove give mod rights to org rank 1 (Knight, Advisor, Board Member, etc.) or higher :
<tab><highlight><symbol>maprank 1 to mod<end>

To remove the special rights for org rank 1 or higher:
<tab><highlight><symbol>maprank rem 1<end>
