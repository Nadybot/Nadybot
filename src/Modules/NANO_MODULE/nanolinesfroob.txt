To show all froob professions that have nanolines:
<highlight><tab><symbol>nlf<end>

To show the froob nanolines for a particular profession:
<highlight><tab><symbol>nlf 'profession'<end>

To show the nanos in a particular nanoline that froobs can use:
<highlight><tab><symbol>nlf 'nanoline'<end>

To show the nanos in a particular nanoline that a froob profession can use:
<highlight><tab><symbol>nlf 'profession' > 'nanoline'<end>
