Startpage is an alternate attempt to present important information
to player. It's meant as the single place to show all notifications
and reminders when players logon or join the private channel.

To show the currently configured startpage:
<tab><highlight><symbol>startpage<end>

To add a tile to the beginning of the startpage:
<tab><highlight><symbol>startpage pick 0<end>

To remove a tile from the startpage:
<tab><highlight><symbol>startpage rem 'tile name'<end>

To see your startpage:
<tab><highlight><symbol>start<end>

To customize the message that is sent with the startpage:
<tab><a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> settings change startpage_startmsg'><symbol>settings change startpage_startmsg</a>
