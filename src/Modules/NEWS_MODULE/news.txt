To show the latest news entries:
<highlight><tab><symbol>news<end>

To add a news entry:
<highlight><tab><symbol>news add 'text'<end>

To pin or unpin a news post:
<highlight><tab><symbol>news pin 'news id'<end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>news unpin 'news id'<end>

To remove a news entry:
<highlight><tab><symbol>news rem 'news id'<end>
