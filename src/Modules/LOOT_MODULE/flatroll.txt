To add an item to the loot list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot add 'item name'<end>
or
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot add 'item reference'<end>

To add an item multiple times to the loot list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot addmulti 'amount' 'item reference'<end>
(e.g.: <symbol>loot addmulti 3 Lockpick)

To add multiple items to the loot list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>mloot 'item reference 1' 'item reference 2', ...<end>
(e.g.: <symbol>mloot <a href="itemref://247707/247707/1">Solid Clump of Kyr'Ozch Bio-Material</a><a href='itemref://270339/270338/77'>Miy's Melee Armor Cloak</a>)

To remove an item from the loot list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot rem 'item number'<end>

To clear the loot list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot clear<end>

To show the loot list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot<end>

To roll the loot list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>flatroll<end>

To re-add remaining items after a roll:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reroll<end>
