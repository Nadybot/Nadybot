<header>Chat Commands<end>

Here you can find an overview of some commands with samples.

<header2>Playtime<end>

<highlight>/played<end><tab>[<a href='chatcmd:///played'>try it</a>]

Tells you for how long you have played this character. Also gives you the local time, both in-game and real-time.

<header2>Date of Birth<end>

<highlight>/born<end><tab>[<a href='chatcmd:///born'>try it</a>]

Tells you when character was created.

<header2>Stucked<end>

<highlight>/stuck<end><tab>[<a href='chatcmd:///stuck'>try it</a>]

When you are stuck, sit down and use this command and you will be moved after 30s.

<header2>Tip of the Day Infowindow<end>

<highlight>/TipOfTheDay<end><tab>[<a href='chatcmd:///tipoftheday'>try it</a>]

Will bring up the next tip.

<header2>Messagebox<end>

<highlight>/messagebox [text]<end><tab>[<a href='chatcmd:///messagebox YAY u have done it'>try it</a>]

Opens a messagebox with [text] in it and a OK button to close it.
