<header2>Hitpoints by Breed<end>

Atrox 4 hitpoints from each point in bodydev
Opifex 3 hitpoints
Solitus 3 hitpoints
Nanomage 2 hitpoints

The amount of healdelta you get is:
base + (bodydev / 100) + healdelta items
Base is 4 for atrox, 3 for opi/sol, 2 for nanomage

healdelta tickrate formula:
(870 - stam) / 30 = tickrate

example (870-810)/30=2 secs
