<highlight><tab><symbol>whatbuffs<end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>whatbuffs 'type'<end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>whatbuffs 'skill'<end>

Use <highlight>whatbuffsfroob<end> or simply <highlight>wbf<end> if you
want to see items usable by or buffs castable on froobs only.
