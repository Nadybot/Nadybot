To calculate the effective casting time of a nano:
<tab><highlight><symbol>nanoinit 'nano attack time' 'nano init skill'<end>

Example:
<tab>Your nano has an attack time (cast) of <highlight>1.2<end>.
<tab>You have <highlight>1200<end> Nano Init skill:
<tab><a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> <symbol>nanoinit 1.2 1200'>/tell <myname> <symbol>nanoinit 1.2 1200</a>
