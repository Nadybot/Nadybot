To calculate your aimed shot recharge:
<tab><highlight><symbol>aimshot 'attack time' 'recharge time' 'aimed shot skill'<end>

If you are lazy, just use
<tab><highlight><symbol>weapon 'drop weapon into chat'<end>

<tab>Your weapon has an attack time of <highlight>1.2<end> seconds and a recharge time of <highlight>1.5<end> seconds.
<tab>You have <highlight>1200<end> Aimed Shot skill:
<tab><a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> <symbol>aimshot 1.2 1.5 1200'>/tell <myname> <symbol>aimshot 1.2 1.5 1200</a>
