To calculate your burst recharge:
<tab><highlight><symbol>burst 'attack time' 'recharge time' 'burst delay' 'burst skill'<end>

If you are lazy, just use
<tab><highlight><symbol>weapon 'drop weapon into chat'<end>

Example:
<tab>Your weapon has an attack time of <highlight>1.2<end> seconds and a recharge time of <highlight>1.5<end> seconds.
<tab>Your weapon has a Burst Delay of <highlight>1600<end>.
<tab>You have <highlight>900<end> Burst skill.
<tab><a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> burst 1.2 1.5 1600 900'><symbol>burst 1.2 1.5 1600 900</a>

<i>Your Burst Delay value (1600) can be found by using the <a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> help specials'>specials</a> command or on <a href='chatcmd:///start http://www.auno.org'>auno.org</a> as Burst Cycle.</i>
