To calculate your full auto recharge:
<tab><highlight><symbol>fullauto 'attack  time' 'recharge time' 'full auto recharge' 'full auto skill'<end>

If you are lazy, just use
<tab><highlight><symbol>weapon 'drop weapon into chat'<end>

Example:
<tab>Your weapon has an attack and recharge time of <highlight>1<end> second
<tab>and a Full Auto recharge value of <highlight>5000<end>.
<tab>You have <highlight>1200<end> Full Auto skill:
<tab><a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> fullauto 1 1 5000 1200'><symbol>fullauto 1 1 5000 1200</a>

<i>Your Full Auto recharge value (5000) can be found by using the <a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> help weapon'>weapon</a> command or on <a href='chatcmd:///start http://www.auno.org'>auno.org</a> as FullAuto Cycle.</i>
