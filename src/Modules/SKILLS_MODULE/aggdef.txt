To find out where to set your aggdef slider to be 1/1:
<tab><highlight><symbol>aggdef 'attack time' 'recharge time' 'init skill'<end>

If you are lazy, just use
<tab><highlight><symbol>weapon 'drop weapon into chat'<end>

Example:
<tab>Your weapon has an attack time of <highlight>1.2<end> seconds and a recharge time of <highlight>1.5<end> seconds.
<tab>Your melee/ranged init is <highlight>1200<end>:
<tab><a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> <symbol>aggdef 1.2 1.5 1200'>/tell <myname> <symbol>aggdef 1.2 1.5 1200</a>
