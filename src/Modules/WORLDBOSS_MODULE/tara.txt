Manage Tarasque spawn times

To list the next spawn time(s) for Tarasque:
<tab><highlight><symbol>tara<end>

To set a new timers once Tarasque has been killed:
<tab><highlight><symbol>tara kill<end>

To manually set a new timer when Tarasque is vulnerable:
<tab><highlight><symbol>tara update 'time'<end>

To delete the timer, because you are not interested in it:
<tab><highlight><symbol>tara del<end>

<header2>Examples:<end>

Tara will be vulnerable in 1 hours, 2 minutes and 10 seconds:
<tab><highlight><symbol>tara update 1h2m10s<end>
