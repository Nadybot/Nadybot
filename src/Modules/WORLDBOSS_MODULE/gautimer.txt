<header>::::: Gauntlet Timer :::::<end>

<highlight>Show current Gauntlet Timer:<end>
<tab><symbol>gauntlet

<highlight>Update Gauntletimer:<end>
<tab><symbol>gauupdate X:Y
<tab><symbol>gauupdate XhYmZs
<highlight>or<end>
<tab><symbol>gauset X:Y
<tab><symbol>gauset XhYmZs
X = hours and Y = minutes and Z = seconds until Vizaresh is vulnerable!

<highlight>Vizaresh killed:<end>
<tab><symbol>gaukill
automatically sets a 17h7m timer.

<highlight>Subscribe for a raid<end>
If you want to subscribe for a raid then simply
use the <symbol>gauntlet command and click the date
and the char you want to participate.

<highlight>Show Bastion tradeskillprocess:<end>
<tab><symbol>gautrade

For manual Subscribequeue roll:
<tab><symbol>gauntlet rollqueue

Normal respawntime is 17h7m
