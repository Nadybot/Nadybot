To show current Gauntlet buff Timer:
<tab><highlight><symbol>gaubuff<end>

To show current Gauntlet buff Timer for a clan side:
<tab><highlight><symbol>gaubuff clan<end>

To set the Gauntlet buff Timer:
<tab><highlight><symbol>gaubuff 'time'<end>

To set the Gauntlet buff Timer for omni:
<tab><highlight><symbol>gaubuff omni 'time'<end>

<header2>Examples:<end>

Set the gauntlet buff to expire in 10 hours and 15 minutes:
<tab><highlight><symbol>gaubuff 10h15m<end>

Set the clan gauntlet buff to expire in 10 hours and 15 minutes:
<tab><highlight><symbol>gaubuff clan 10h15m<end>

See <a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> help budatime'><symbol>help budatime</a> for info on the format of the 'time' parameter.
