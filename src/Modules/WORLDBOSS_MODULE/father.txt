Manage Father Time spawn times

To list the next spawn time(s) for Father Time:
<tab><highlight><symbol>father<end>

To set a new timers once Father Time has been killed:
<tab><highlight><symbol>father kill<end>

To manually set a new timers when Father Time is vulnerable:
<tab><highlight><symbol>father update 'time'<end>

To delete the timer, because you are not interested in it:
<tab><highlight><symbol>father del<end>

<header2>Examples:<end>

Father Time will be vulnerable in 1 hours, 2 minutes and 10 seconds:
<tab><highlight><symbol>father update 1h2m10s<end>
