Manage Loren Warr spawn times

To list the next spawn time(s) for Loren Warr:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loren<end>

To set a new timers once Loren Warr has been killed:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loren kill<end>

To manually set a new timers when Loren Warr is vulnerable:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loren update 'time'<end>

To delete the timer, because you are not interested in it:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loren del<end>

<header2>Examples:<end>

Loren Warr will be vulnerable in 1 hours, 2 minutes and 10 seconds:
<tab><highlight><symbol>loren update 1h2m10s<end>
