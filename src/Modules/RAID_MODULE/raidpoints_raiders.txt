To check your current raid points at <myname>, use
<tab><highlight>/tell <myname> points<end>

To see a list of the most recent points you got at <myname>, use
<tab><highlight>/tell <myname> points log<end>

To see a list of the most recent points you got at <myname> on any of your alts, use
<tab><highlight>/tell <myname> points log all<end>

Checking your points only works in tells to prevent others from seeing
how many points you have.


To get a list of people with the most raid points, use
<tab><highlight><symbol>points top<end>
